INTRODUCTION:
DevOps is an important approach in modern software development that combines development and 
operations to create a faster, more reliable, and efficient process. Learning DevOps helps 
developers understand the full lifecycle of software — from coding to deployment and maintenance.

IMPORTANCE OF LEARNING DEVOPS:
- Helps connect the development and operations teams.
- Makes it easier for teams to work and communicate together.
- Speeds up how fast software is made and delivered.
- Uses automation to save time and reduce manual work.
- Finds and fixes problems faster.
- Improves the quality and reliability of software.
- Supports continuous testing and delivery of updates.
- Makes the workflow smoother and more organized.
- Teaches useful skills needed in today’s tech jobs.
- Helps bring new ideas and updates quickly.
